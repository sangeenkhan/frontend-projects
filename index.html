About Me

I am Sangeen Khan, a front-end architect and digital designer dedicated to building immersive, high-performance web experiences. I transform complex ideas into elegant, user-centric interfaces that blend technical precision with creative vision.

With a deep passion for modern UI/UX paradigms, web animations, and clean architecture, I bridge the gap between design and development. My focus is on crafting digital products that are not only visually striking but also highly functional and responsive across all platforms.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring emerging web technologies, experimenting with 3D interfaces, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in a browser.
