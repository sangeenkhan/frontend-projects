Our Heritage

A Legacy
Crafted in Gold

Born in the bustling heart of Riyadh, Layali is more than a restaurant; it is a sanctuary of Saudi heritage. We bridge the ancient spices of the Silk Road with the refined techniques of modern fine dining.

Every dish tells a story of the desert, the oasis, and the sea. From the saffron-infused rice of the Najd to the freshly caught seafood of the Red Sea, we source only the purest ingredients to create an unforgettable global experience.
